About the Author

Selam Van Voorhis

Selam immigrated to America from Ethiopia after being adopted at six years old. She led a strong academic journey from participating in the Goddard Scholars Program at Sullivan Middle School, the Technology and Engineering Academy at Doherty Memorial High School and eventually selected as a Boston Posse and National Girls Inc. scholar being awarded over $180,000 to attend Centre College for computer science. Since graduating this past May, she has been hired by Girls Inc. of Worcester as the development and donor relations coordinator. Her future aspirations include becoming an entrepreneur of a global brand providing digital content, tools, and aid.
